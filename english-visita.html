Visita
 • exchange program

Visita was an exchange program between the organizations Comuna, from Rio de Janeiro and Agora, from Berlin. The project was conceived as a trip for experiential learning in collective management practices of autonomous cultural spaces. For this, Comuna's team occupied Agora's facilities and developed activities in the fields of music, food, publishing and performing arts. This project was included in the open call "Intercâmbio e Difusão Cultural da Secretaria de Cultura do Rio de Janeiro".

My role I created the proposal of the project and was in charge of its executive production and logistics.
