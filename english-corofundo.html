–coro–fundo–
 • platform

–coro–fundo– is a platform created to foment Rio de Janeiro's independent electronic music scene and its agents. Through the elaboration of projects (on and off the dance floor) that articulate concepts such as visibility, locality and documentation, –coro–fundo– seeks to discuss the artistic, social and political practices linked to the specific construction of a "Rio" scene. In its first year of existence, –coro–fundo– held parties and festivals, produced documentaries, workshops and other educational activities. More information about the projects below in (–coro–fundo–Festival, Recreo and MyGirlfriend).

My role I founded and built the platform's overall vision. I also act as its director, researching and developing the concepts that are articulated in its different projects while also leading the artistic curatorship of its events.
