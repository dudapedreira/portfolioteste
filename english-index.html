Professionally, I act within my greatest vocational interest: thinking and creating infrastructure of independent art scenes. It is a dynamic practice that involves bridging from contemporary global critical thinking (media and network studies, economics, art and technology), conceptual frameworks and ideas that enable a local cultural engineering. My curiosity and inspiration arises from the symbolic and aesthetic values that scenes create, my investigations are about the structural advances and innovations that might strengthen them.

I believe that in building self-sufficient art projects, the borderlines that distinguish work positions and roles are sometimes beneficially forced to move. So, whether creating or directing initiatives and campaigns, playing music or assembling speakers, I've been involved, for the past decade, in the development of the projects described on this page. If it is of interest, I am available to work and collaborate in the fields of project design and strategy; networks and cultural agents articulation and in the research and speculation of new artistic practices. Furthermore, I am always open to connect. : )
